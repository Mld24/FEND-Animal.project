Swallow

The swallow is called the "bird of freedom" because it can not be take in captivity.

  • Scientific Name: Hirundinidae
  • Avarage Length: 15-20 cm
  • Average Weight: 18 g
  • Average Speed: 50-65 km/h

Swallow are good flying because of the aerodynamic body, so they have adapted to hunt on the wing. The nest is made of a mixture of earth, saliva and grass.
